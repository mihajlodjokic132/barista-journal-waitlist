Why Barista Journal

Built by a coffee enthusiast, for daily espresso practice.

Barista Journal started as a personal tool to stop forgetting what actually worked. After hundreds of home shots, it became a focused system for getting more consistent, more confident, and more intentional behind the machine.

  • Remember what worked: Shot notes stay tied to dose, yield, and time.
  • Improve faster: Patterns are visible so fixes become obvious.
  • Brew with confidence: Practical guidance is available while you make drinks.
  • Make every bag count: Track bean performance and reduce wasted coffee.
